What's the primary goal of the homepage?

This determines the layout, content hierarchy, and call-to-action

A

Lead Generation

Drive visitors to get in touch / request a quote. Hero + value props + strong CTA ("Get Started", "Book a Call"). Conversion-focused.

B

Brand & Credibility

Establish Altitecture as a serious player. Showcase expertise, process, and results. Portfolio/case studies prominent. Trust-building first, CTA second.

C

Product Showcase

Demonstrate the AI-powered building process itself. Interactive demos, before/after, live previews. "See what we can build" as the hook.
