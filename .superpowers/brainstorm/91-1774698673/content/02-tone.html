What tone should the homepage convey?

This shapes the copy, imagery, and overall feel

A

Technical & Precise

Speak to CTOs and engineers. Clean, data-driven, minimal. "We build enterprise-grade software with AI precision." Feels like the Stitch reference design.

B

Bold & Visionary

Speak to founders and decision-makers. Ambitious, forward-looking. "The future of software is built different." Big statements, striking visuals.

C

Approachable & Clear

Speak to non-technical buyers. Friendly, jargon-free. "Tell us what you need. We'll build it." Focus on simplicity and outcomes over process.
